## Overview

Todoist Advanced is a leading task management application that combines natural language task entry with powerful project organization, priority management, and cross-device sync to keep your work and personal responsibilities organized and actionable.

Natural language input lets you type tasks the way you think about them — including date, time, and priority information embedded in the task text — with Todoist interpreting the due date and scheduling the task automatically without separate form fields.

Project hierarchy with sections, subtasks, and nested organization accommodates the structure of complex work, allowing projects to be broken down to any level of detail while maintaining a clear view of progress and remaining work at each level.

Karma productivity scoring tracks your task completion patterns over time, providing a motivating feedback loop that makes consistent completion visible and rewards productive habits with tangible progress metrics.

For individuals and teams who need a reliable, cross-platform task management system that is simple enough to use consistently but powerful enough to organize complex workloads, Todoist Advanced is the task manager that millions of users have made the center of their productivity practice.

## Performance

Todoist balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
